Key to Neotropical Triplophyllum

Based on Prado, J. & Moran, R.C. 2008. Brittonia 60: 103-130. Multi-entry key format developed by Gabriela Zuquim.

Triplophyllum glabrum

Basal pinnae Basiscopic side Strongly elongated, Size more than 0.6 times the length of the lamina. Indument in the adaxial surface of the VEINS glabrous or with hairs, in the INDUSIA Glabrous, in the LAMINA MARGIN eciliate (without hairs), number of cells 1 to 5, size 0.1 - 0.2 mm. In the LAMINA BETWEEN VEINS ABAXIALLY glabrous, ADAXIALLY glabrous. In the PINNA RACHIS ADAXIAL surface of pinna rachis Glabrous. Hairs on the ABAXIAL surface of pinna rachis Number of cells 1 to 5, size smaller than 0.5 mm long.
